A little about me!

I've always been intrigued with how we communicate with computers and how fast technology evolves, just to think how far computers and the internet have come since the 60's is absolutely astonishing!

I became facinated with computers when I was in high school. My senior year I went back to school two weeks early to help out in the computer lab. Classmates even paid me to upgrade their Myspace pages for them once they started allowing users to enter their own HTML. I guess that was my first, under the table, Front End job.

Background

I graduated from UNC at Pembroke in 2012 with a bachelors in Information Technology. I began studying Front End Development a few years later while working in the IT department for the company that I have been with for the last three years. I am now looking to make the transition from IT to Front End Development as that is what I truly enjoy. And like they say, if you enjoy what you do, you never work a day in your life.
